Tournament Draw

This guide is intended as assistance for manually drawing the cup stage after the preliminary group stage for the Norwegian "Norgescup" ahead of the 2023-2024 season. If your tournament format is not supported please use the default one in cuescore.com. Go "Back" and press "Info" for more info on how the draw is made.

Step 1: Select the correct tournament format below (number of groups and qualifiers).

Step 2: Put in all the names of the players (required fields are marked) and press calculate.

Step 3: Now go back to the tournament page (cuescore.com), press "manual draw" and fill in exactly as calculated (including the order of the matches).
